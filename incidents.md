# Incidents

To open an incident you can either:

- Directly email support [support@trmregistry.com](mailto:support@trmregistry.com)
- Open a GitHub Issue

    Make sure to follow the [Contributing guidelines](CONTRIBUTING.md#finding-the-right-project)

## Priority support

If you [upgrade your plan to PRO](https://trmregistry.com/plans), one of the benefits is priority support and direct contact with the maintainers ([Regesta S.p.A](https://www.regestaitalia.eu/)/[Clarex S.r.l](https://www.clarex.it/)).

Contact sales for more details.
